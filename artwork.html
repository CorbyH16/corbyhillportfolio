This is my submission for my P3DE module. My 3D model features a small, stylised robot with a glowing lightbulb attached to its head and I presented it in a minimal, softly lit room to enhance the lightbulb. The simple geometry and clear design make it ideal for games or animation, whilst the warm lighting emitting from the bulb as well as the eyes and where the legs attach to the body adds personality and a strong visual focus.
